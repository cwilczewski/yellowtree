Since 2005 Yellow Tree Inc. has been designing and manufacturing custom cabinets. For over 10 years we have developed our expertise to work on custom projects just as unique as yours. We are committed to helping our customers create the household space of their dreams. At Yellow Tree Inc we pride ourselves on delivering the true custom look you are searching for, while offering the widest selection of styles, features and finishes that make it "Absolutely You".

The first thing you'll notice is that we love what we do. We love the art of cabinet making, and we stop at nothing to ensure our clients are beyond satisfied. This is why we strive to bring the latest in cabinet designs, materials and production process to the GTA area market place.
